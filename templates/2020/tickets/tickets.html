Tickets



How much should I pay?

Our tickets for Pycon Africa 2020 follow a ‘pay what you can’ model. However, we would like to encourage you, to support our event and community as best you can.

If you would like to support Pycon Africa, please consider donating any amount you can. You are also welcome to reach out to our team if you would like to become a special sponsor or support the conference.

Where your support goes:
Any extra proceeds from the conference will go into our grants budget, which we will use to fund financial aid for the next edition of Pycon Africa. This allows us to make the conference as accessible as possible to a wide range of Pythonistas from across the continent and the world.

Personal: ≥ USD0.00
If you are using Python as a freelancer, a student, for personal projects, a hobby and more we encourage you to support us any way you can.

Business: ≥ $USD100
If a company is paying for your ticket, kindly support by purchasing the business ticket.

Payments:
Payments can be done during your registration, you can find the link to that below.
